ABOUT THIS PROJECT

Welcome to Data Wars, an interactive atlas tracking police surveillance technologies across the United States. This platform visualizes the rapid expansion of surveillance infrastructure and its impact on civil liberties.

Our database includes:

  • Detailed records of surveillance technologies deployed by law enforcement agencies
  • Vendor information and procurement documents
  • Geographic distribution of surveillance networks
  • Temporal analysis of technology adoption

The data reveals patterns in the militarization of police forces and the normalization of surveillance in everyday life.
